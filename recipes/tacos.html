Tacos

From south of the border a taco is a meat cilantro and onions combo that will please everyone

spicy and citrus wil get your taste buds dancing!

Ingredients

  1. Steak
  2. onions/li>
  3. cilantro
  4. tortilla
  5. lime
  6. taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. cook steak
  2. chop onions and cilantro
  3. add seasoning to steak
  4. assemble taco
  5. lime to taste
