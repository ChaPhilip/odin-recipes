Fried eggs

a quick breakfast

fried eggs is a classic. fried eggs can be eaten as is or made into a sandwich. a dash of hot sauce can spice up the eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 4 tsp oil
  • pinch of salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. heat pan add oil
  2. crack eggs into pan
  3. cook for 2mins on medium
  4. once the whites are cooked remove from heat
  5. add salt and pepper to taste
